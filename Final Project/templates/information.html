What is cryptocurrency?

Basic information

Cryptocurrency, sometimes called crypto, is any form of currency that exists digitally or virtually and uses cryptography to secure transactions. Cryptocurrencies don't have a central issuing or regulating authority, instead using a decentralized system to record transactions and issue new units. (source)

Facts about cryptocurrencies

  • There are over 18,000 different crytocurrencies.
  • Dogecoin was started as a joke.
  • The real identities of some of the creators of cryptocurrencies still seem to be unknown.
